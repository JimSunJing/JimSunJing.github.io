Ferma Zitella的英文翻译和歌词大意（Taranta Project）乐评

Ferma zitella Ludovico Einaudi

英文翻译由youtuber“AmariSziAmari”提供

Ferma zitella ca si carcerata

mo ci t’aggiu 'ncuntrata sola sola

e sola sola, e mo sola sola

mo’ ci t’aggiu 'ncuntrata sola sola

mo sola sola.

(The Man)

Stop yourself spinster who are jailed

now that i met you all alone, all alone…all alone…

now that i met you all alone.

Lassame scire nennu, pe la strada

ca su zitella e perdu la furtuna

mo’ la furtuna, mo’, la furtuna

ca su zitella e perdu la furtuna

(The Spinster)

Let me go my sweetheart, by the street

because i’m a spinster and i could lose the dowry

i mean the dowry, i mean, the dowry

Vieni stasera alla mia camerata

la mia mamma non c’è, me corcu sola

me corcu sola, me corcu sola

la mia mamma non c’è, me corcu sola

en: (The Spinster)

Come tonight at my bedroom

my mother isn’t there, i’m lying down alone

i’m lying down alone, i’m lying down alone

my mother isn’t there, i’m lying down alone

E bonasera a lei porta nserrata

pacenzia Nennu e ca non su sola.

e ca nu su sola, e ca nu su sola.

pacenzia Nennu e ca non su sola.

(The Man)

So good evening to it, locked door!

(The Spinster)

Be patient my sweetheart now 'cause i’m not alone

Because i’m not alone, now, i’m not alone

Tutte le curpe io ca t’aggiu lassata

quandu t’aggiu ncontrata,

ma sola sola, mo sola sola, e mo sola sola

quandu ca t’aggiu ncontrata, mo sola sola.

(The Man)

All my fault that i’ve left her

when i met her all alone

but all alone, really, all alone

when i met her all alone

这首歌大概有三个情节，第一个是男女主人公在大街上遇见，可能是一见钟情。由于女人家教非常严格，所以男人用“jailed”来形容她的处境。接着女人强调现在是在大街上，让他放他走，会失去嫁妆这一句我不太懂，是在比喻她的“名誉”和“贞洁”吗？接着女人说，今夜她母亲不在家里，我独自入睡（暗示男主过来）

接着是男人在女人家门口的情节，男人到达之后发现门是锁的，女人解释道：亲爱的请你耐心一点，现在我并不是独自一人（也许是父母在家，又也许是她有了另一位情人？）

第三个情节是男人的独白，后悔在和女人独自见面时把她放走。
