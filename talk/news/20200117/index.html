Is ‘Mindhunter’ Canceled? The Netflix Show May Not Be Coming Back for Season 3, and Fans Aren’t Happy

origin from cheatsheet.com by Megan Elliott
time: 2020/1/16

We may have seen the last of Holden Ford and Bill Tench. The future of Mindhunter is in doubt, Deadline is reporting. Netflix has released the cast from their contracts and executive producer David Fincher is busy with other projects. That means that season 3 of the dark series about a pair of FBI agents and a psychologist who study serial killers is on hold — at least for now.

我们可能已经追完了Holden Ford&Bill Tench(MindHunter中的双男主)的故事。Deadline报道提出：MindHunter的未来走向成谜。Netflix已经解除主演的合同，其监制大卫芬奇也正忙于其他企划。这意味着这部讲述两个FBI探员和一位心理学家研究连环杀人犯的黑色剧集第三季将被暂缓。

David Fincher is working on other Netflix projects

大卫芬奇正忙于其他的Netflix项目

David Fincher had a big hand in creating the distinctive look and feel of Mindhunter. And he directed four episodes in season 1 and three in season 2. But now, he’s focused on other projects.

大卫芬奇在这部拥有独特的影像质感的《心灵猎人》的创造中发挥了很大作用(had a big hand in)。他导演了第一季中的4集剧集，第二季中的3集剧集，但现在的他正专注于其他项目。

“David is focused on directing his first Netflix film Mank and on producing the second season of Love, Death and Robots,” a Netflix spokesperson said in a statement to Deadline.

“大卫芬奇正专注于他的第一部Netflix电影《曼克》以及《爱，死，机》的第二季制作。”一位Netflix发言人在Deadline 的报道中说到。

Mank is a biopic about legendary Hollywood screenwriter Herman J. Mankiewicz, who wrote Citizen Kane. Love, Death, and Robots is an animated science-fiction anthology series.

《曼克》是一部关于曾写过《公民凯恩》的好莱坞编剧Herman J. Mankiewicz的传记片。《爱死机》是一个科幻动画短片选集。

Fincher didn’t want to prevent the show’s cast from pursuing other opportunities

芬奇不希望阻碍到剧集的演员们的事业发展

With Fincher too busy to work on Mindhunter Season 3 right now, he felt it was only fair to let the cast, which includes Jonathan Groff as Ford, Holt McCallany as Tench, and Anna Torv as Wendy Carr, pursue other opportunities.

由于芬奇现在太忙碌而无暇续拍《心灵猎人》第三季，他觉得让该剧集的主演去把握其他新机会对他们更加公平。

“He may revisit Mindhunter again in the future, but in the meantime felt it wasn’t fair to the actors to hold them from seeking other work while he was exploring new work of his own,” Netflix explained in a statement.

“他以后可能会再回来制作《心灵猎人》，但现在他觉得自己在制作新作品而主演们由于他的原因工作暂缓是不公平的。”Netflix在一份声明里解释到。

The cast would like to return

主演期望剧集回归

While there are no firm plans for Mindhunter Season 3, Deadline reports that the cast would be happy to return to the show. McCallany has also tweeted several articles clarifying the show has not been canceled and thanking fans for their support.

虽然现在暂时没有更进一步的计划，Deadline报道指出该剧的主演非常愿意回归该剧。Holt McCallany（在剧中饰演Tench大叔）在推特发表了几篇文章表示该剧没有被取消，并感谢粉丝们的支持。

Mindhunter Season 2 was released in August 2019, nearly two years after the premiere of season 1. While Netflix does not release viewership data for its shows, the show was well-received by both fans and critics. But the second season made less of a splash than the first. However, it has a loyal fanbase, which could affect the streaming service’s willingness to move forward with season 3, Deadline noted.

《心灵猎手》第二季于2019年8月放出，距离第一季的放出将近两年。虽然Netflix没有放出该剧的收视率数据，通过一些评分网站可以看出该剧受到不少观众和评论家的欢迎。但是第二季的反应没有第一季轰动。虽然如此，该剧拥有非常忠诚的粉丝群体，这会影响到流媒体的意愿去推动第三季的拍摄。
